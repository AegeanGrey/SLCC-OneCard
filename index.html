What is a OneCard?

OneCard provides students at SLCC with amenities such as access to buildings (Lifetime Activities Center, Markosian Library, Labs, etc.), campus funded accounts with Bruin Bucks to get discounts on available services offered around campus, and access to UTA Transit Pass!

Having a OneCard can also prove to be beneficial for students who engage with college events and activities by being able to vote for student council members for SLCCSA. SLCC Students will also be able to attend Athletic events for free or at a discounted price.

Online

SLCC does have a in-depth guide for how to get a OneCard Online you can find here.
This will be referenced for the steps below

What you will need:

  • A headshot style photo of yourself
    • Only includes your face and shoulders
    • Must not contain hats, sunglasses, pets or other items
  • A Government-issued ID (Driver's License, Passport)
    • Used for identity validation purposes
    • SLCC will discard it after being verified

What to do:

  1. Email the following information to OneCard@slcc.edu

    • Full Legal First and Last Name
    • Preffered Name
    • Pronouns (optional)
    • Student Number
    • Home Address
    • Email Address (either Personal or Bruin Mail)
    • Phone Number
    • Location you'd like to pick your OneCard up at:
      • Taylorsville-Redwood ID Center
      • South City Campus ID Center
      • Jordan Campus ID Center
    • Attach both your headshot photo and screenshot of chosen government ID

  2. Wait 24-hours for SLCC to verify the information you've provided.

  3. Once completed, your OneCard will be made available for pickup at the ID Center you've provided in the email. Please refer to OneCard Location & Hours for available times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to pay for my OneCard?

A: No, your OneCard fee is already covered by your student fee's.

Q: Do I need a OneCard to access the UTA Pass?

A: Yes, you will need a OneCard to access the UTA Pass.

Q: Do I need to be an active student at SLCC for UTA Pass?

A: Yes, students need to be enrolled in at least 1 class at SLCC for the UTA Pass to remain active.
If a student drops below the 1 course requirement then the UTA Pass will be deactivated.

Q: Can my UTA Pass be deactivated if it isn't being utilized?

A: Yes, the UTA Pass will be deactivated if it isn't used within 6 months.
Students will need to reach out to the OneCard office by phone to reactivate.

Q: What does the UTA Pass cover?

A: The UTA Pass can be used on the FrontRunner, TRAX (Blue/Red/Green Line Trains), UTA buses, and S-Line Street Car.

Q: What does the UTA Pass not cover?

A: The UTA Pass does not cover express buses, ski resort buses, or other special UTA transportation.
